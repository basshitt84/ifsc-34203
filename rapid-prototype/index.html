About

OrOrOr (pronounced only “Or” when spoken aloud) is comprised of founding two-man songwriting nucleus Everett Hagen and Jack Lloyd — who sing, play guitar and play and program synths and beats — alongside three other integral musicians: Mike Motley on drums, Ryan Hitt on bass and Norman Williamson on saxophone, all of whom are local mainstays. The music these five make together is a swirling, shifting brand of nearly unclassifiable electronic-infused rock music.
